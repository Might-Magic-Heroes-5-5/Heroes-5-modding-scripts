Other heroes from Ashan (H3, H4, H6) are added to sum up a total of 18 per faction, each of which has unique specialization and is evenly balanced among others for competative gameplay (refer to "Hero index" ).  Heroes are devided evenly in 3 classes Might, Balanced and Magic. Might and Magic dominate in the field related to their name while Balanaced is a mix of both.
